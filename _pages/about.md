## Overview

I am a first-year PhD candidate at [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.ust.hk/), advised by Professor [Junxian He](https://www Homepage.ust.hk/~junxianhe/). I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) with a B.Eng. in June 2024.

## Research Interests

My research focuses on **natural language processing** and **machine learning**. Specific areas of interest include:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**: Exploring how large language models can be trained and evaluated for complex reasoning tasks
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**: Investigating and mitigating hallucination issues in vision-language models
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**: Understanding how to make LLMs more truthful and interpretable
